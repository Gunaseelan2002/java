Welcome to Madhu Typewriting Institute Typewriting exams are conducted by Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Tamil Nadu, once in six months. The grades of certification are Pre-Junior, Junior, Senior and High-speed levels. Students, who have completed sixth standard in their school level, can appear for pre-junior exam. For appearing in Junior, students must have completed eighth standard. Students, who complete junior exam, can of course, type words ranging from 30 words per minute for Junior and it is 45 words per minute for Senior.
